# Sources: 10 Experts on Cold Outreach for B2B SaaS

Curated list of 10 practitioners who publish in public. Each entry will eventually have POV, verified recent material, and collection path. Starting with the shortlist + channel reality check.

## Selection filter

1. Practitioner, not pundit. Runs a team, agency, or product where advice gets tested.
2. Publishes frequently in the last 6 to 12 months.
3. Differentiated POV.
4. Shares receipts (numbers, scripts, teardowns).
