Research base for building an opinionated playbook on cold outreach in B2B SaaS.

## Why this topic

One of the 8 options I had on the list. Cold outreach for B2B SaaS has high density of practitioners who actually publish in public. So there's real material to work with instead of pundit takes.

## Selection filter

1. Practitioner, not pundit. Runs an outbound team, agency, or product where advice is tested weekly.
2. Publishes frequently in the last 6 to 12 months.
3. Differentiated POV.
4. Shares receipts: numbers, scripts, teardowns.
